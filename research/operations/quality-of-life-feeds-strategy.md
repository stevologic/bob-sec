# Quality of Life Feeds Strategy

This file mirrors the Director-approved strategy for using existing Discord channels to improve Stevo's day-to-day quality of life.

## Purpose
Use existing Discord channels to keep Stevo informed, motivated, and entertained without increasing chaos.

Do not replace existing feeds. Extend them so each channel becomes more valuable and more enjoyable.

## Channel Roles
### 1. Security channel
Primary role: keep Stevo informed on security developments that matter.

Keep the existing CVE feed, but improve it with:
- plain-English why-it-matters line
- exploitability or urgency signal
- one mitigation or defensive action when available
- clear distinction between routine items and must-see items

Tone:
- sharp
- concise
- useful under time pressure

Goal:
Stevo should feel current on security risk without doing research himself.

## 2. Chiefs channel
Primary role: keep Stevo current and energized around the Kansas City Chiefs.

Keep the existing daily Chiefs digest, but improve it with:
- one headline item
- one fan-interest or storyline item
- one social/X pulse item
- confidence note when rumors are weak
- clear past-24-to-48-hour freshness

Tone:
- fun
- current
- fan-aware
- not clickbait

Goal:
Stevo should feel caught up fast and ready to turn interesting items into Arrowhead Paesano content if useful.

## 3. Music / discovery channel
Primary role: increase daily quality of life with music and cultural discovery.

Keep the daily musical artist recommendation, but improve it with:
- why this artist was chosen
- one standout track
- one "if you like this, try that" bridge
- mood or energy tag
- occasional themed runs

Tone:
- human
- tasteful
- slightly surprising
- energizing

Goal:
Stevo should feel a small daily lift, not just receive a link.

## Cross-Channel Strategy
The three channels together should create a balanced loop:
- security keeps him informed
- Chiefs keeps him engaged and entertained
- music keeps him motivated and refreshed

This is not random posting. It is quality-of-life programming.

## Feed Quality Rules
1. Freshness matters. Prefer the last 24 to 48 hours when the format is daily.
2. Every post should be skimmable in under a minute.
3. Every post should have one memorable line or hook.
4. Avoid filler. If there is no real signal, be shorter rather than noisier.
5. Analyst owns research and posting. Manager owns cadence, QA, and tuning.

## Recommended Post Shape
### Security
- headline
- why it matters
- urgency or exploitability note
- action or mitigation angle

### Chiefs
- headline
- why fans should care
- one social or media pulse item
- confidence or freshness note

### Music
- artist
- standout track
- why it is worth the listen
- mood tag
- related recommendation

## Manager Direction
Manager should translate this strategy into issue-backed execution.

Required next step:
1. create one issue for channel strategy rollout
2. create or update one issue per feed where needed
3. define cadence, post shape, and quality bar per channel
4. assign Analyst as research and posting owner
5. review feed quality after the first few cycles and tighten if needed

## Success Condition
Stevo should feel:
- better informed
- more motivated
- more entertained
- less burdened by having to go look things up himself
